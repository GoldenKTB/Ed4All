SOLUTION


Our project, Ed4All, aims towards achieveing the targets of the Sustainable Development Goal 4 - Quality Education. Ed4All does so by eradicating the language barriers to ease and suit a student's learning needs. A student can choose the language he/she is fluent or comfortable with and the rest will be taken care by Ed4All. It assists you with taking down important notes during lessons, daily tasks to be completed, assignments to complete and much much more!
