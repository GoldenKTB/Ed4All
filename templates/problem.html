WHAT IS THE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING?

As rightly stated by Nelson Mandela, “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” But unfortunately many people do not have access to learning. One of the main reasons why is due to LANGUAGE BARRIERS. People find it difficult to communicate and gain knowledge about important and compeling worldwide cases/events. But we have shown enough sympathy and it is time we start eradicating this horrible disease. This is the reason why, we have come up with Ed4All; We build an EDvantage.

To summarize, our Problem Statement was: How can we reduce illiteracy rates and provide Education for all?

Some of the reasons for an increase in Language Barriers is an inadequate amount of resources and lack of access to Technology.
