WHAT IS THE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING?

As rightly stated by Nelson Mandela, “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” But unfortunately many people do not have access to learning. Due to this, THOUSANDS do not have the basic knowledge on occupations and how to build their own successful career. This leads to countless challenges and difficulties in a person's life. But we have shown enough sympathy and it is time we start eradicating this horrible disease. This is the reason why, we have come up with Ed4All; We build an EDvantage. To summarize, our Problem Statement was: How can we reduce illiteracy rates and provide Education for all?
